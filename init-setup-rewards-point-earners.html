Set your meal times

If your restaurant has multiple meal times or menus, you may want to set different items to display as point earners for each menu. For example, you might have a lunch menu that only runs from 11AM to 3PM on Mondays through Fridays that has items not offered at any other time.

Below, click Add Meal Time to create a new menu (lunch, dinner, late night, etc...)

If you only have one menu, just click the next button.
