I am having trouble logging into my Jetti Account.

When you signed up for with Jetti, you provided us with a PRIMARY EMAIL ADDRESS. This is the email that Jetti sends all communications to. Take a second to look in your inbox for this account. You should see an email titled Jetti Registration Code. In that email you should see a 10 DIGIT REGISTRATION CODE and a link to the Jetti Admin Login Page.

Copy the REGISTRATION CODE and click on the link to the Admin Login.

When you are at the Login Page, enter your PRIMARY EMAIL ADDRESS and paste your code in to the fields. Click ‘LOG IN’.

You should be taken to a new page that asks you to set up a new password.

If you were not, click here to have another registration code sent to your email address.

If you need further help contact Jetti here: help@jettisystems.com.

I can login but I cannot set my new password.

When you first log in to you Jetti Admin Account, Jetti asks you to create a new password know only to you (even we don’t know it). This, along with your PRIMARY EMAIL ADDRESS, is what you will use to log into Jetti on return visits.

On the Set Your Password Screen, if you are getting errors when you try to advance to the next page it could be the result of a few things.

First, make sure that you have entered your new password in both fields.

Second, make sure that both of these passwords match EXACTLY. Jetti passwords are case-sensitive so make sure you are using the correct upper and lowercase letters.

If you need further help contact Jetti here: help@jettisystems.com.

I can't login in AFTER I’ve entered my registration code and changed my password.

If, after you have registered your account and changed your password, you have trouble logging to your account try these steps.

Make sure that the email you are entering is your Primary Email Account. This is the email that Jetti sends all of our communications with you to.

Make sure that your password is correct including the correct usage or upper and lowercase litters (Jetti Passwords are case-sensitive).

If you can’t remember your email click here.

If your can’t remember your password click here.

If your can’t remember who you are, call a doctor. :)

If you need further help contact Jetti here: help@jettisystems.com.

You can access your profile from any page by clicking on the ‘Profile’ link in the top right of the Jetti Admin.

What are the format requirements for my logo image?

When uploading a logo for your restaurant on the Profile Page, your image must be in the following format:

  • No larger that 250px by 250px
  • GIF or PNG format
  • Transparent Background
  • 72dpi (for quick downloads)

If you do not have an image in this format you can use a program like PhotoShop or an online image optimizer such as http://www.imageoptimizer.net.

If you need further help contact Jetti here: help@jettisystems.com.

What are the format requirements for my Home Screen background image?

When uploading a Home Screen background image for your restaurant on the Profile Page, your image must be in the following format:

  • No larger that 1024px (height) by 764px (width)
  • GIF, PNG or JPG format
  • 72dpi (for quick downloads)

If you do not have an image in this format you can use a program like PhotoShop or an online image optimizer such as http://www.imageoptimizer.net.

If you need further help contact Jetti here: help@jettisystems.com.
