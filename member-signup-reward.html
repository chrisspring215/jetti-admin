Sign-Up Reward (Optional... yet recommended)

By providing a small incentive to get your customers to sign-up for you’re your loyalty rewards program, your can greatly increase its usage and profitability. Below you can provide an optional ‘gift’ that the customer can redeem on their next visit to your establishment.

To activate this feature, click the switch below and enter in a sign-up reward. It is recomended that you offer a percentage discount rather than an item since you can not garuntee when the customer will be coming in again.
