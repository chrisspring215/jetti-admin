Set Your Point Earners and Reward Items

OK, so here is the really cool stuff that makes you more money.

Jetti allows you to create categories within the menus that you just created like ‘drinks’, ‘appetizers’, ‘entrees’ and ‘desserts’. You then add items to these categories that earn you the most money, move a poorly performing item, or promote a new dish or drink. It’s up to you. Your guests earn extra points when they order these items; points that can be redeemed in the future for free items of your choosing.

The best way to implement this is to add items that have a high profit margin as your Point Earners and set low cost items as Reward Items.

First select one of your menus that you created, then create some categories inside that menu (you can have up to 4 catergories)
